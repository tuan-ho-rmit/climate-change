OUR MISSIONS

Public Awareness
We hope to raise the public's awareness on current global warming reality. By offering comprehensive data for everyone, we aim to show global warming in the most accurate way and promote clean practices to the environment for a greener future.
Promote transparency
Our mission is to provide a platform where scientists, policymakers, and the public can access accurate and relevant statistics to better understand the complex relationships between population and temperature, and the reality of today's global warming.
Sparking change
We aim to create a positive shift in attitude and behavior towards the environment. By delivering insightful data on climate change, we hope to influence everyone to make more conscious decisions about the world, for a better tomorrow.
